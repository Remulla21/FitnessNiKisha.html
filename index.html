Balance fitness refers to the ability to maintain a stable, upright position while standing or moving. It's a crucial component of overall physical fitness, especially as we age, impacting our ability to perform everyday tasks and reducing the risk of falls. Balance training can improve stability, coordination, and reduce the likelihood of injuries.
